\section{Female Sex Worker Data}\label{app.model.fsw}
Raw data from two FSW surveys in Eswatini
--- 2011 \cite{Baral2014} (N = 325) and 2014 \cite{EswKP2014} (N = 781) ---
were analyzed to support parameterization of FSW within the HIV transmission model,
including substratification of lower \vs higher risk FSW.
